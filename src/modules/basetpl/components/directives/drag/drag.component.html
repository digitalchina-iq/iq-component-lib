概述

该指令使宿主元素可被拖动

drag, dragX, dragY

三个指令选一个即可，drag：可拖动x轴和y轴，dragX：可拖动x轴，dragY：可拖动y轴

<div style="position: relative;width: 200px;height: 200px;background: #ccc"> <div dragY style="width: 40px;height: 40px;background: blue;"></div> <div dragX style="width: 40px;height: 40px;background: green;"></div> <div drag style="width: 40px;height: 40px;background: red;"></div> </div>

stopDrag

停止拖拽

<div style="position: relative;width: 100px;height: 100px;background: #ccc"> <div drag [stopDrag]="stop" style="width: 30px;height: 30px;background: red;"></div> </div> <button class="m-btn-success" (click)="stop=!stop">{{'{'}}{{'{'}}stop?'置为可拖拽':'置为不可拖拽'{{'}'}}{{'}'}}</button>

dragStart,dragMove,dragEnd

拖拽过程中的事件，开始、过程、结束

<div style="position: relative;width: 100px;height: 100px;background: #ccc"> <div drag (dragStart)="start($event)" (dragMove)="move($event)" (dragEnd)="end($event)" style="width: 30px;height: 30px;background: red;"></div> </div> start(e){{'{'}} console.log(e); {{'}'}} move(e){{'{'}} console.log(e); {{'}'}} end(e){{'{'}} console.log(e); {{'}'}}
